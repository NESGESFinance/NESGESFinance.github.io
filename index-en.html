Proof-of-Production (PoP) & Oracles

Summary

The PoP system captures productive metrics (plastic tons, fuel liters, CO‚ÇÇ tons avoided, kWh generated, units produced) and records them every 30 days into an index anchored to Bitcoin transactions (Taproot commitments) and IPFS contents for public auditability.

Oracles

  • Production oracle (internal) ‚Äî feeds the PoP index.
  • Environmental-impact oracle ‚Äî measures CO‚ÇÇ avoided and energy consumption.
  • Social-verification oracle ‚Äî audits beneficiaries and deliverables.

Governance and Audit
