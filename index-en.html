Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to the most common questions about our ecosystem.

Is NESGESFinance a financial entity or investment fund?

No. We are a Society of Collective Benefit and Interest (S.A.S. BIC) that designs and operates technological infrastructure for tokenizing real projects. We are not a bank, investment fund, securities issuer, nor do we capture public savings.

What are the costs to tokenize a project with you?

For projects selected in the Alpha Phase, we cover infrastructure development costs. Projects must cover their own operational costs. In later phases, we will implement a cost-sharing model based on generated value.

Do I need blockchain technical knowledge to participate?

Not necessary. We provide all technical infrastructure, development, and integration. Your team can focus on what they do best: operating the real project. We provide continuous training and support.

How is custody of tokenized assets managed?

We implement 3/5 multisig custody (S.A.S. + external auditor + strategic partner) with semiannual key rotation. All movements are verifiable on-chain and registered in public log (IPFS + blockchain).

What type of projects are ideal for tokenization?

Projects with: 1) Verifiable physical location, 2) Real production/operation flows, 3) Positive social/environmental impact, 4) Committed team, 5) Interest in radical transparency, 6) Demonstrable economic viability.
